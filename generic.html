Our Projects

OculusTheta

Oculusθ is our new innovation that we are developing. The impacts of this technology are that this technology will limit the amount of time patients spend in surgery, thus reducing risk. Furthermore it will result in a more efficient, easier surgery. This will benefit both the patient and the surgeon.

OculusNano

OculusNano is our smaller development that will have a large impact. The nanotubes will electronically detect the cell type that they are in contact with. This enables them to sense if any bacterial or inflammatory cells start to arise. If this is the case, they will send out a radio signal. This can be received by an external detector, and upon first signs of an infection, antibiotics can be prescribed to halt infection early.​ Reducing the amount of surgeries that would have to take place. ​
